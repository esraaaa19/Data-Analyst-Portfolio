# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company and identifies the key factors contributing to customer attrition.

## Objective

The goal of this project is to understand why customers leave the company and provide insights that can help improve customer retention.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## KPIs

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Active Customers
- Churn Rate (%)
- Average Monthly Charges
- Average Customer Tenure

## Dashboard Features

- Customer Demographics Analysis
- Churn by Contract Type
- Churn by Internet Service
- Churn by Payment Method
- Monthly Charges Analysis
- Customer Tenure Analysis

## Key Insights

- Customers with month-to-month contracts showed the highest churn rate.
- Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to leave.
- Electronic check users had higher churn compared to other payment methods.
- Higher monthly charges were associated with increased customer churn.

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Offer loyalty benefits for customers with low tenure.
- Improve retention strategies for high-risk customer segments.
- Introduce personalized offers for customers likely to churn.
